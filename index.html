How does this work?

Your browser subscribes and parses MQTT JSON data from the house's weather station.

Time readout

This tracks GPS time, it should be accurate even if the Internet goes out.

Readout intervals

The readout depends on the configuration of the ESP32 weather station, readouts are usually every 15 seconds.
